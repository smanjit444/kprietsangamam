ABOUT US

We conduct various unique themed art contests

Graffio, the Art and Craft club of KPRIET aims to bring out the creative mind in each and every individual. The mission of this club is to extend cultural enrichment to the community at large by exhibiting the artists' works at various venues around college to stress the value and importance of art in the world. Graffio club conducts unique art contests and webinars and encourages students to take part in it. The club's ultimate motto is appreciation and creation of fine art.
