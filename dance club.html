ABOUT US

We encourage students to follow their passion for dance

Dance club KPRIET encourages students to exhibit and explore their talent in dance, with a motto of "Dance not to impress, but to express". The dance club helps students to overcome stage fear and to explore their hidden talents with a view of bringing them to spotlight.
