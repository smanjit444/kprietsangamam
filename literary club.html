ABOUT US

We conduct exciting events with a view of developing english language skills

The Literary Club of KPRIET aims in making each and every participant a brave speaker. The motto of this club is to break the thought "English is Difficult". The mission of literary club is to enrich the english knowledge and to develop communication in an entertaining way. The club helps shaping students as professionals by training them to write formal letters, mails, reports and more. It also makes learning english fun, by conducting events like connection, dumbcharades and so on thus making the students to gain english knowlwdge in an easier way.

"The english language is a work in progress.Have fun with it."
