ABOUT US

We spread social awareness messages through our mime acts

The Mime Club of KPRIET aims to popularize the word mime around the society and create an awareness on benefits of mime. The vision of this club is to make students a competent communicator by using eyes, facial expression, as well as whole body. It molds the members to get out of fear and self-doubts about their stage plays. By imparting a variety of events and plays, the mime club enhances the body language skills and improves the confidence of the students by motivating them.

"Mime makes the invisible,visible and the visible,invisible"

- Marcel Marceau
