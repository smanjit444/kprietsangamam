ABOUT US

We help students understand politics and procedures happening in the parliment

The vision of Youth Parliament KPRIET is all youths should know politics. Today's youth is tomorrow's India. Therefore, each youth must know the happenings and politics involved in those happenings. The benefit of the Acts and schemes passed in the Parliament will be discussed among the students through unique activities. The club also trains students to be a good leader. It's mission is to make sure that politics is for all
